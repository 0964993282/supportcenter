Adding/removing managers

  1. Visit the “Managers” tab from your hackathon management area.
  2. Click the “Add manager” button.
  3. Enter the email address or screen name associated with the user’s Devpost account. Click “Add”.
  4. This user is now added as a manager. They’ll receive an email confirmation with a link to access the hackathon management area.
  5. To remove a manager, click the “remove” link to the right of the user’s name in the list of managers. The user will not receive an email that they have been removed, but will no longer be able to access the hackathon management area.
