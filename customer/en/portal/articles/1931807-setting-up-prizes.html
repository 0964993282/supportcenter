Setting Up Prizes for Your Hackathon

This article explains the relationship between setting up prizes on the Edit hackathon tab and awarding prizes to winners at the end of the hackathon.

Setting up prizes on the “Edit hackathon” tab

You can define the prizes for your hackathon by navigating to the Edit hackathon tab and then Prizes. To set up additional prizes, simply click Add Prize. If you are including opt-in awards or sponsor prizes, you can select the check box before clicking Save Prize. The prize will be automatically added to your hackathon's submission form and the submission gallery filters.
