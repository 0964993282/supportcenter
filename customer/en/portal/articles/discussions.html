Discussions

Any Devpost user can post a question or comment on the hackathon Discussions tab. This area is frequently used to post a public question to the hackathon managers.

Users can opt-in to receive an email when new discussion topics or comments are added. By default, any user who posts a topic or comment will receive future email alerts on that thread.

When a manager replies to a thread, they’ll be indicated with a “MODERATOR” tag next to their name in the comments.
