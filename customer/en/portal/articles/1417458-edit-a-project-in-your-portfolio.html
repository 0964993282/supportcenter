Edit a project in your portfolio

You can access and work on the software in your portfolio at any time.

  1. Navigate to devpost.com.
  2. Log in to your Devpost account.
  3. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.
  4. Select the project that you want to edit.
  5. Under your project’s title, click the link labeled “Edit project.”
  6. Make any desired changes and click “Save” at the bottom of the page.
