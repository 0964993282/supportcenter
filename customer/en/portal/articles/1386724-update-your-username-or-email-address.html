Devpost Help Center

You can update your username and email address at any time:

  1. Log in to your Devpost account with Facebook or GitHub, or use your existing email address.
  2. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.
  3. From the dropdown menu, select “Settings.”
  4. Select “Account” to edit your username and email address, or to connect your GitHub and Facebook accounts.
  5. Click “Save changes.”

Please note: if you update your email address, expect a new confirmation email from us. You won't be able to access your account until you click the link in the confirmation email.
