Hackathon homepage

The homepage contains most of the info participants need to know about a hackathon.

  • Left column:
  • Intro video (optional) – some hackathons contain a video to promote the event
  • Tagline and description – the tagline is a short summary of the goal of the hackathon, and the description below provides more details
  • Eligibility – describes who can participate
  • Requirements – describes the rules around what participants will be asked to create
  • How to enter – the steps a user needs to take to submit
  • Judges – who will decide the winners
  • Judging criteria – what the judges will use to compare the submissions
  • Right column:
  • “Call to action” (CTA) box – this box changes based on the state of the hackathon. It displays the next action the user can take (e.g., register, submit a project, vote on submissions, etc.), the time remaining in the current period (e.g., 42 days left to submit), and sharing options to invite others to participate
  • Prizes – how many winners will be awarded, and what they’ll receive
  • Sponsors (optional) – managers have the option to add sponsor logos here
