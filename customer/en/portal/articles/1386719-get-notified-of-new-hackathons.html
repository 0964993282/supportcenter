Devpost Help Center

Here's how to get notified when we launch new hackathons in your area:

  1. Log in to your Devpost account.
  2. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.
  3. From the dropdown menu, select "Settings."
  4. Select "Notifications."
  5. Select "send me Devpost's weekly newsletter featuring career advice & new hackathons."
  6. Click "Save changes."
