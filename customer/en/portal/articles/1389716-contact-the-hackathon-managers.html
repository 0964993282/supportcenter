Contact the hackathon managers

If you have general questions about a hackathon, you can post to the Discussion Forums or you can click on "Email the hackathon manager" from their Devpost hackathon page if an email address was provided:

OR After you have submitted to a hackathon, you can contact the hackathon managers through your submission. To do this, log in to your Devpost account and follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the hackathon’s home page.
  2. Click the blue “Manage your submissions” button.
  3. Click on the gray “Message a manager” button beneath the submission you are inquiring about.
  4. Type your message and click “Add Message.”

Please note, messaging a manager through your submission means that the conversation is private and your email address will not be shared.
