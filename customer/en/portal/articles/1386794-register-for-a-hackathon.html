Register for a hackathon

Before you can enter a submission or access the submission form, you must first register for the hackathon you’re interested in.

Please note: signing up for a Devpost account does not register you for any hackathons.

  1. Navigate to the URL of the hackathon you’re interested in. If the submission period is open, you will see a "Register for this hackathon" button.

  2. Click the "Register" button. (Once you are registered, that button will change to say "Enter a submission".)
  3. Complete the pop up survey to help hackathon managers learn how you heard about the hackathon and if you're planning to submit.
