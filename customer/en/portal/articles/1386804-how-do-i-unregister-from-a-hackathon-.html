Unregister for a hackathon

You can unregister for a hackathon at any time up until you submit a project.

  1. Log into your Devpost account
  2. Navigate to the hackathon you'd like to unregister from
  3. Click the "Unregister" link below the "Look for teammates" button

  4. Once you unregister from a hackathon, you'll no longer receive email updates about it and you won't be able to submit a project to the hackathon.
