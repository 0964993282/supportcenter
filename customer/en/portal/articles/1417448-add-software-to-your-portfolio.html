Adding software to your portfolio

Before you can add software to your portfolio, you must first sign up for and confirm a Devpost account.

To add software to your portfolio:

  1. Navigate to devpost.com.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.
  4. Click the yellow box that’s labeled “Add a project to your portfolio.”
  5. Complete all of the required fields on the form and click “Save” at the bottom of the page.

You can access and work on the software in your portfolio at any time. The exception is hackathon submissions, which you can work on only until the submission deadline.
