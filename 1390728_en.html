To vote, you’ll need to sign up for a Devpost account. If you don’t have an account, sign up for one before voting.



1. If you haven’t already, log in to your Devpost account.


2. Navigate to the URL of the hackathon you want to vote in.


3. Select the tab labeled "Submission Gallery."


4. Select the submissions you want to vote for. This will bring you to the submission page.


5. Click the “Vote” button on the right side.

After your vote has been counted, the button will change from "Vote for this submission" to "Thanks for voting!"
