Yes, here’s how to delete software from your portfolio:



1. Navigate to devpost.com.


2. Log in to your Devpost account.


3. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.



4. Select the software that you want to edit.

5. Click the blue button labeled "Edit project."

6. Click the link at the bottom of the page labeled “Delete project.”


Please note: deleting software from your portfolio cannot be undone, and it is not possible to delete challenge submissions.
