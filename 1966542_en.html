Are you having trouble formatting your text? When you copy and paste from Microsoft Word, a lot of invisible formatting codes are inserted and can cause formatting problems. Typical symptoms include being unable to change the formatting of some pieces of text, or nothing happens when you use formatting buttons, or your content looks messy and doesn’t retain your original formatting.



The best way to work around this is to do the following:

1. Copy and paste your text from Microsoft Word into a plain text editor. This will remove all the invisible formatting codes.

2. Copy and paste the plain text into our text box.

3. Format the text within our text box.


If you’ve never used a plain text editor, we recommend TextPad on PC and TextEdit on Mac.
