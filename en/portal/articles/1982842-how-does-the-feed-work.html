How does the feed work?

If you are logged in to your account, your feed will be the first page you see when you head to devpost.com.

Your feed displays content based on the people you’re following. Follow your favorite hackers to keep up with new projects they’re working on, hackathons they’ve registered for, interesting people they’re following, and awesome projects they’ve liked.

Not sure who to follow? Find people on Devpost you already know in the Find Friends section by connecting your Facebook, Twitter, or GitHub account.
