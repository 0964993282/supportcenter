I can’t upload my files

For problems uploading to the "Images" field:

Make sure that your image is one that we support (JPG, GIF, or PNG files no larger than 5MB). If your images meet these requirements and you're still having trouble, contact us.

For problems uploading to the "Upload a file" field:

Make sure that your file type is one that we support (Word documents, PDFs, and ZIP files no larger than 35MB). If you're still having trouble, contact us.
