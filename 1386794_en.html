Before you can enter a submission or access the submission form, you must first register for the hackathon you’re interested in.


Please note: signing up for a Devpost account does not register you for any hackathons.
 

To register for a hackathon:



1. Navigate to the URL of the hackathon you’re interested in. If the submission period is open, you will see a "Register" button.
