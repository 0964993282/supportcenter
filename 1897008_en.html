You can now customize your portfolio by adding a header image or changing the background color.


To customize your portfolio:


1. Navigate to devpost.com.

2. Log in to your Devpost account.

3. Click your username at the top right corner of the page to go to your Portfolio.


4. Click the “Edit Header Design” link on the bottom right corner of your portfolio header.


5. You can now upload an image (JPG, PNG, or GIF) or select a color for your header background.
Once you are done, click the “Save Header Design” button.
