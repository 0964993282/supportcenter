Your public profile shows the software you've entered in hackathon and personal information that you may have entered upon sign-up, such as your photo, location, bio, Twitter handle, website, or skills.
 

Please note: we will never make your email address or full name public. For more information on what will be shown to the public, please see our privacy policy.
 

To update your profile information:


1. Log in to your Devpost account.


2. Click your username at the top right corner of the page.


3. From the dropdown menu, select "Settings."


4. Select "Info" to edit your photo, name, location, bio, Twitter handle, or website. Click "Save Changes" after you’ve updated your information.
